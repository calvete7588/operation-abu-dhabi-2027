## Objective
Understand the foundational concepts of cybersecurity, the core mission of a Security Operations Center (SOC), and identify organizational assets.

## Core Concepts Explained
* **Cybersecurity:** The practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. Its main goal is to ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.
* **SOC (Security Operations Center):** A centralized team of security professionals that monitors, detects, and responds to cybersecurity incidents in real-time.

## Technical Vocabulary
* **Security** (/si-kiú-ri-ti/): Protecciones aplicadas a un sistema.
* **Asset** (/á-set/): Activo. Cualquier elemento de valor para la empresa (datos, servidores, laptops).
* **Threat** (/zret/): Amenaza. Cualquier peligro potencial que intente dañar o acceder a un activo.
