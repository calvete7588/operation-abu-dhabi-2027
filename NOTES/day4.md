**Date:** July 9, 2026

## Objective
Understand the CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability) and how it applies to protecting critical organizational assets.

## Core Concepts Explained
* **Confidentiality:** Ensuring that data is only accessible to authorized users. Prevent data leaks.
* **Integrity:** Ensuring that data remains accurate and unaltered by unauthorized parties or system errors.
* **Availability:** Ensuring that systems and data are reliable and accessible to authorized users whenever needed.

## Technical Vocabulary
* **Confidentiality** (/con-fi-den-shi-á-li-ti/): Confidencialidad.
* **Integrity** (/in-tég-ri-ti/): Integridad.
* **Availability** (/e-vei-la-bí-li-ti/): Disponibilidad.
