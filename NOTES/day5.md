## Day 5 – Security Frameworks, Controls, and Ethics
**Date:** July 10, 2026

## Objective
Understand the importance of Security Frameworks (like NIST CSF) to develop security procedures, classify controls, and analyze the ethical and legal responsibilities in cybersecurity.

## Core Concepts Explained
* **Security Frameworks:** Structured guidelines used to develop processes and establish clear goals to protect organizations and people from cyber threats.
* **Security Controls:** Specific safeguards implemented to mitigate risks. Categorized into Physical (locks/cameras), Technical (software/firewalls), and Administrative (policies/training).
* **NIST CSF & CIA Triad:** Key frameworks used universally to manage and reduce risk effectively.
* **Security Ethics:** The critical responsibility of protecting privacy, maintaining strict confidentiality, and strictly complying with data protection laws.

## Technical Vocabulary
* **Framework** (/fréim-uork/): Marco de trabajo.
* **Security Controls** (/si-kiú-ri-ti con-tróuls/): Controles de seguridad.
* **Compliance** (/com-plái-ans/): Cumplimiento normativo o legal.
