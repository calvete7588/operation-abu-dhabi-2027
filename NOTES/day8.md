## Objective
Master the core frameworks (NIST CSF, NIST SP 800-53), review CIA triad alignment, study OWASP secure design principles, and understand the internal security audit lifecycle.

## Core Concepts Explained
* **CIA Triad:** Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability — the foundational pillars of information security.
* **NIST Frameworks:**
  * *NIST CSF:* High-level strategic framework for managing cybersecurity risk.
  * *NIST SP 800-53:* Detailed catalog of security and privacy controls for federal systems and enterprise infrastructure.
* **OWASP Secure Design Principles:** Best practices (e.g., Least Privilege, Defense in Depth) used to build security directly into systems and software.
* **Internal Security Audits:** Formal reviews conducted to evaluate compliance, assess control effectiveness, and provide evidence for risk mitigation.

## Technical Vocabulary
* **Audit** (/ó-dit/): Auditoría / Evaluación formal de controles.
* **Defense in Depth** (/di-fens in dep-ts/): Defensa en profundidad (capas de seguridad).
* **Least Privilege** (/liist pri-vi-ledsh/): Principio de menor privilegio.
