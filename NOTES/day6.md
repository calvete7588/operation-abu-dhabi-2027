## Day 6 – Threat Actors and SOC Core Tools (Module 4)
**Date:** July 12, 2026

## Objective
Understand different threat actors and gain a core baseline comprehension of the primary tools utilized by SOC Analysts (SIEM, Playbooks, Packet Sniffers, Linux, SQL, and Python).

## Core Concepts & Tools
* **Threat Actors:** Classified adversaries including Cybercriminals, Hacktivists, Insider Threats (like the disgruntled employee scenario), and Nation-States.
* **SIEM Tools (Splunk & Chronicle):** Security Information and Event Management systems used to aggregate logs, analyze data, and perform daily monitoring tasks.
* **Playbooks:** Structured step-by-step guides that dictate exact operational procedures during incident response.
* **Packet Sniffers:** Network protocol analyzers used to capture and inspect live data packets traversing the network.
* **Core Technical Stack:** Introduced **Linux** operating system operations alongside **SQL** (database querying) and **Python** (security automation scripting).

## Technical Vocabulary
* **Threat Actor** (/zret ác-tor/): Agente de amenaza.
* **SIEM** (/sim/): Security Information and Event Management.
* **Packet Sniffer** (/pá-ket sní-fer/): Analizador de paquetes de red.
